Site Collection Administrators

Site collection administrators are the users who have permissions over all the content in Site Collection. They have ultimate control over everything in the site collection that can be managed in the web interface. Site Collection Admins can manage the functionality of features such as Search, the Recycle Bin, Document ID, and more. Site admins are users that have permission to manage sites, including any subsites. They don't need to have an admin role in Microsoft 365, and aren't given access to the SharePoint admin center. In your tenant/environment, you might have one user in charge of one site collection and another in charge of another site collection. So might be a good way to separate administrative responsibilities.

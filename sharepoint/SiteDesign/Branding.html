Branding

In the modern SharePoint experience, you can easily change the look and feel of your site to match your company or organizational brand You can customize the logo, colors, and navigation – often without writing a single line of code. Branding can be applied at the site level, to a group of sites, or to all sites within your organization

Home Page Design

SharePoint includes an updated set of default site themes that are responsive and look great on any device. With site themes, you can customize your site's logo and colors to match your brand. Site templates provide specific layouts and other functionality for your site. Additional branding can be achieved using custom themes or site templates without worrying about something breaking when SharePoint is updated

You can quickly and easily customize the look of your SharePoint site to reflect your professional style and brand. The options available to you depend on the version of SharePoint you're using.
